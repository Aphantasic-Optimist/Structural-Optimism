Welcome! This is your first step into understanding structural optimism.

## The Core Idea

Reality is structured toward integration. When things come together in the right way, they create something more than the sum of their parts.

## Why This Matters

Understanding this structure helps us:

- Make better decisions
- Build stronger communities
- Find meaning in our lives
- Work toward positive outcomes
