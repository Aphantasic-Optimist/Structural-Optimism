## Summary

Holt-Lunstad et al. (2010) meta-analysis of 148 studies with 308,849 participants found social connection reduces mortality risk by 50% (OR=1.50, 95% CI 1.42-1.59). Effect comparable to smoking cessation, exceeds obesity/physical inactivity. Replicated across cultures.
