# social connection effect exceeds obesity and physical inactivity effects

**Type:** Entity

## Summary

Holt-Lunstad et al. (2010) meta-analysis of 148 studies found social connection's mortality risk reduction (50%, OR=1.50) exceeds effects of obesity and physical inactivity, comparable to smoking cessation. Established across cultures.
