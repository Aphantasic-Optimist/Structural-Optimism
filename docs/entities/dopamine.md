## Summary

Neurotransmitter that acts as accelerator for reward-seeking behavior, signals when outcomes exceed expectations, promotes immediate action and approach behavior. Has opposing effects to serotonin in decision-making and learning. Released in nucleus accumbens through oxytocin-VTA pathway during social interactions, creating positive feedback loop that drives approach behavior.
