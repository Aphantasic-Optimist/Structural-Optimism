# Core Claim: Social Connection Reduces Mortality

## Content

Social connection reduces all-cause mortality by approximately 50%. This is based on a meta-analysis of 148 studies with over 300,000 participants (Holt-Lunstad et al. 2010). The effect size is comparable to quitting smoking.
