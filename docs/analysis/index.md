## The Core Problem

### We Lost the Middle

Algorithms optimize for engagement, not connection. Engagement is highest when content:
- Confirms your beliefs (feels good)
- Outrages you (also engagement)

So algorithms show you things you already agree with, or things that make you angry.

**Never:** Nuanced views. Bridge-builders. People you slightly disagree with.

**Result:** We lost the MIDDLE. We lost the BRIDGE. We lost "WE."

## The Fragmented Landscape

### Information Ecosystems

People live in different information ecosystems:
- You see content from sources you trust
- You unfollow sources you distrust
- Networks become increasingly separated
- Each cluster has different "facts"

**We don't know what others know.**

**We don't even know we're in separate ecosystems.**

## The Mental Health Crisis

### Documented Impacts

- 85% of young people (16-25) report concern about climate change
- 45% say it affects their daily functioning
- Record levels of depression, anxiety, suicide
- Political disengagement: "My vote doesn't matter"

### The Cause

400 years of free will denial culminating in:
- Algorithmic determinism ("You're predictable")
- Economic determinism ("There is no alternative")
- Climate doom ("It's too late")
- Technological determinism ("AI will replace you")

**Result:** Paralysis. If you have no agency, why try?
