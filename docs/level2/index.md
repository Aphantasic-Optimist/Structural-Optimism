## The Health Evidence

### Connection Predicts Survival

**Meta-analysis of 90 cohort studies (Wang et al. 2023):**
- 2.2 million people across multiple countries
- Social isolation increases mortality risk by 32%
- Loneliness increases mortality risk by 14%
- Effects comparable to smoking, obesity, physical inactivity

**Religious community participation (VanderWeele et al. 2016):**
- 89,708 women followed for 14 years
- 20-30% lower depression rates
- 5-fold lower suicide rates
- Effects independent of social support alone

**Physiological mechanisms:**
- Reduced inflammation
- Improved cardiovascular function
- Enhanced immune response
- Better stress regulation

This isn't psychological—it's biological. Connection literally keeps us alive.
