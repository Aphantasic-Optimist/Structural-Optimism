## Why This Matters

### Independence Enables Honesty

**Without external funding:**
- No pressure to conform to donor interests
- No obligation to support specific agendas
- No risk of funding withdrawal for controversial claims
- No need to please sponsors
- No conflicts of interest

**This enables:**
- Radical honesty about evidence
- Willingness to correct errors
- Freedom to follow evidence wherever it leads
- Ability to criticize powerful interests
- Independence from institutional pressures

### No Financial Incentives

**This project does not:**
- Sell books or courses
- Charge for access
- Require membership fees
- Accept donations
- Monetize content
- Seek profit

**Why?**
- Keeps focus on truth, not profit
- Prevents exploitation of vulnerable people
- Avoids financial conflicts of interest
- Maintains independence
- Models non-commercial knowledge sharing

### Sustainability Model

**How is this sustainable without funding?**

**Current model:**
- Author self-funds (personal time and resources)
- GitHub provides free hosting (GitHub Pages)
- MkDocs Material is open source (free)
- Community contributes voluntarily (no payment)
- No ongoing costs beyond time

**This works because:**
- Static site (no server costs)
- Open source tools (no licensing fees)
- Volunteer contributions (no payroll)
- Simple infrastructure (minimal maintenance)
- No profit motive (no growth pressure)

**Limitations:**
- Author has limited time
- Updates may be slow
- No paid staff
- No marketing budget
- Organic growth only

**Trade-off:** Slower growth, but complete independence.

## Transparency Commitments

### What We Disclose

**We commit to disclosing:**
- All funding sources (currently: none)
- All financial relationships (currently: none)
- All conflicts of interest (currently: none)
- All costs and expenses (currently: ~$15/year)
- Any changes to funding model (if they occur)

**We will disclose:**
- Who funds us (if anyone)

- What strings are attached (if any)
- Why we accepted (reasoning)
- How it's used (accounting)

**We will NOT:**
- Hide funding sources
- Accept anonymous donations
- Obscure financial relationships
- Minimize conflicts of interest
- Downplay financial influences

### How to Verify

**You can verify our independence by:**
- Checking GitHub repository (all public)
- Reviewing commit history (all changes visible)
- Examining content (no commercial bias)
- Asking questions (we'll answer)
- Monitoring for changes (we'll announce)

**If you suspect undisclosed funding:**
- Open a GitHub issue
- Ask for clarification
- Demand transparency
- Hold us accountable

**We welcome scrutiny.**
