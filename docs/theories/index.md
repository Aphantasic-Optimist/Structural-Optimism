## The Honest Approach

These theories acknowledge:

- **Correlation â‰  causation** - Neural correlates don't explain mechanism
- **Timescale problem** - Quantum effects (femtoseconds) vs. neural processing (milliseconds)
- **Alternative explanations** - Classical mechanisms may be sufficient
- **Replication matters** - Single studies are preliminary, not conclusive

**We track speculative ideas, we don't dismiss them.** But we're honest about evidence quality.

## How to Read These Theories

1. **Check the confidence rating** - Know what's established vs. speculative
2. **Look for falsification criteria** - What would disprove the claim?
3. **Verify primary sources** - Are citations to actual papers or interpretations?
4. **Separate physics from interpretation** - What does the research actually say?
