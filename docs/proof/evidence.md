## Health Science

### Social Connection & Mortality

| Study | Sample | Finding |
|-------|--------|---------|
| Wang et al. 2023 | 2.2M people | Social isolation +32% mortality |
| Holt-Lunstad et al. 2010 | 308,849 | 50% increased survival with connection |
| VanderWeele et al. 2016 | 89,708 women | Religious community: 5x lower suicide |

**Key insight:** Connection predicts survival as strongly as smoking, obesity, or exercise.

## Anthropology

### Love is Universal

**Jankowiak & Fischer 1992** examined 166 cultures using the Yale Human Relations Area Files.

**Finding:** Romantic love present in the vast majority of cultures—even those that prohibit its expression.

**Evidence:** Love songs, elopement, personal anguish for beloved found across all cultural contexts.

**Implication:** Love is not a Western invention. It's a human universal.
